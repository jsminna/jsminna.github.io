About JSMinna Internship

Become an intern with JavaScript Minna community to learn and connect to life changing opportunities

You will improve your JavaScript skills and build projects while surrounded by connections, support and opportunities

It's Free

You owe us nothing, except your time, dedication to the project, commitment and passion.

IT'S COLLABORATIVE AND ENGAGING

You will learn and build projects through pair programming, mentor support, guidance and code reviews.

OPPORTUNITIES

You will have a chance to work with an NGO to build a project as well other connections to global and remote jobs after gaining experience building real life projects.

ELIGIBILITY AND REQUIREMENTS

  • A working laptop.
  • Access to the Internet.
  • A Github Account.
  • Slack app on desktop or mobile.
  • Ability to commit to the program for two months.
